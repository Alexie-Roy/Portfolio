About Me

My name is Alexie Roy and I am passionate about making a positive impact and helping others. I enjoy learning new things and have a strong drive to achieve my goals. During my sophomore year of college, I was unsure of what path to take with studying psychology and information technology. That's when I came across the field of UX Design.  After doing research on UX Design, I understood how important this field is with connecting with users and creating a product design that build a rapport among individuals and empowers them.


Through my experience working on UX projects from both internship and college courses I took, I was able to engage in working on web design, perform user experience research to gain data and analyze it, and perform website audits and SEO editing. Not only do engaging in UX Design helps with connecting with users, it helps with creating a meaningful product design that benefits the users and organization. Because of this, it has become my passion to follow this career path into the UX Design field.  I want to be able to give users a product design that will make them feel valued and have a positive impact on their life.
